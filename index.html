About

ReConnect seeks to bridge the gap between refugees in transition and their host communities.

By connecting refugees to a friend in their host community, we hope to provide them with the opportunity to build relationships and learn more about their new home. We believe that providing them a space to foster a relationship gives them more than a friend and information, and also help create an intercultural space that can be a micro, but decisive step towards creating more refugee-welcoming communities.

If you are a refugee in transition, welcome to our community! We are a network of volunteers, students and refugees around the world working to help you be more prepared for your new home after you have received your notice of relocation.

If you are a student and are considering to join our network of friends from around the world, feel free to reach out to us and ask any questions! We are looking for proactive and energetic students who are excited to share their knowledge and guide their refugee friends and make them feel welcome in their new home - needless to say that there is nothing as fulfilling as seeing your friend walking out of the airport gate once they arrive at your country.

Refugee

Welcome to your new community!

Moving to a new country is challenging. What is the food like? How is the weather? What is the history of this place?

We want to help you feel prepared for this transition! Once you sign up, you will be connected to a friend who lives in your new host community, who will teach you about the local culture and language and more! Most importantly, they will be your friend for all the occasions!

Host Friends

As a host friend, your role is to support your refugee friend as he or she prepares to be relocated to your country. In many cases, you will be the only contact he or she has upon arrival in your country.

Requirements

  • Be currently enrolled in a partner university
  • Be willing to devote 1-2 hours a week to your refugee friend
  • Demonstrate empathy, proactiveness and good spirit
  • Complete all training modules
